数据说明Data Indicators

置信度与口径策略说明Confidence Dots & MCU Strategy Badges

由于 11 家公司的年报披露详细程度不同，MCU 营收数据通过不同方式获取，颜色点和策略徽章帮助您快速判断数据可信程度。 Because annual report disclosure depth varies across companies, MCU revenue is sourced in different ways. Color dots and strategy badges help you gauge data reliability at a glance.

置信度圆点（MCU 营收列旁边）Confidence Dots (next to MCU Revenue figures)

绿色 — 直接披露Green — Directly Reported
青色 — 推算（高置信）Cyan — Derived (High Confidence)
橙色 — 估算（低置信）Orange — Estimated (Low Confidence)
红色 — 数据缺失Red — Data Unavailable

绿色和青色数据可直接用于决策参考；橙色数据建议结合公开资料交叉验证；红色表示该年份暂无可用数据。 Green and cyan can be used directly for reference; orange warrants cross-checking with public filings; red means no data is currently available for that year.

常见问题FAQ

常见问题解答Frequently Asked Questions

毛利率为什么很多格子显示「—」？Why do so many gross margin cells show "—"?
+
部分公司（中微半导、峰岹科技、芯海科技、钜泉科技、乐鑫科技）的毛利率暂未覆盖，数据获取中；其余公司毛利率来自年报或 AKShare 财务数据接口，近年逐渐补全。上市前年份（招股书来源）的毛利率因格式未规范，暂未提取，显示「—」。 Gross margin for 5 companies (Cmsemicon, Fortior, Chipsea, Hi-Trend, Espressif) is being sourced. For others, data comes from annual reports or the AKShare API and fills in gradually. Pre-listing years (prospectus source) don't have standardized gross margin disclosure, so they show "—".
MCU 营收和总营收有什么区别？What's the difference between MCU Revenue and Total Revenue?
+
总营收是公司全部业务的营业收入；MCU 营收仅统计 MCU 相关产品线。例如兆易创新同时有 NOR Flash 业务，普冉股份有 SRAM/NOR Flash，乐鑫有模组业务，这些均不计入 MCU 营收。MCU 占比列显示两者之比。 Total Revenue covers all business lines; MCU Revenue is the MCU-specific product line only. For example, GigaDevice also has NOR Flash, Puya has SRAM/NOR Flash, and Espressif has module revenue — none counted in MCU. The MCU Mix% column shows the ratio.
纳思达（002180）的数据为什么没有总营收？Why does Nasda (002180) have no Total Revenue?
+
纳思达集团总营收约 264 亿元（主业为打印耗材），与 MCU 分析无直接关联，故不显示。仪表盘仅展示其极海微电子子公司的「芯片」产品线数据，作为 MCU 营收的参考口径。 Nasda Group's total revenue (~¥26.4B, mainly printer consumables) is unrelated to MCU analysis and is excluded. The dashboard only shows its Geehy subsidiary's "chip" product line as the MCU revenue proxy.
如何比较多家公司在同一年的表现？How do I compare multiple companies in the same year?
+
① 在图表顶部选择目标年份，点击「按年份」视图可横向对比当年各家；② 主表格切换到目标年份，点击「MCU 营收」列表头排序；③ 查看 YoY 热力图该年所在竖列，颜色越深增速越快。 ① Select the target year at top, switch to "By Year" chart view to compare all companies side by side; ② In the main table, select the year and sort by MCU Revenue; ③ Check the heatmap column for that year — deeper color = faster growth.
数据更新频率是多少？How often is data updated?
+
每年 4 月年报季后更新全年数据，覆盖当年财年报告。页面右上角「数据截至」日期显示当前数据版本。临时补录（如毛利率、历史员工数）会随时推送。 Data is updated every April after annual reporting season. The "Data as of" date in the top-right shows the current version. Ad-hoc patches (e.g. gross margin, historical headcount) are pushed as they become available.
如何切换中英文显示？How do I switch the display language?
+
点击仪表盘右上角的 EN 按钮即可切换为英文；点击 中 切回中文。切换后公司名称、图表标题、图例、详情面板内容均同步翻译，偏好保存在浏览器中，刷新后保持。 Click the EN button in the top-right of the dashboard to switch to English; click 中 to return to Chinese. Company names, chart titles, legends, and the detail panel all switch instantly. The preference is saved in your browser and persists on refresh.
